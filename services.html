Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a logo design take? +
A basic logo design is typically completed within 24-48 hours. Complex brand identity packages may take 3-5 business days depending on revisions.
What files will I receive? +
You will receive all source files including PNG, JPG, PDF, SVG, and AI/PSD files depending on the package. You'll own all rights to your design.
How many revisions are included? +
Basic plan includes 2 revisions, Standard includes 5, and Premium includes unlimited revisions until you are 100% satisfied.
How do I make payment? +
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major payment methods. 50% advance and 50% on final delivery for larger projects.
Can you work with clients outside Ludhiana? +
Absolutely! We work with clients across India. All communication and file delivery is done digitally via WhatsApp and email.
